RiSE App- JEE & AIPMT

Preparing for Entrance Examinations? Supplement your preparations with numerous topic-wise, part-wise & Full syllabus tests prepared by expert IITians RISE enables you to improve the skills required to ace competitive exams like JEE (Mains/Advanced), BITSAT, JKCET, etc by letting you practice from a comprehensive set of topic-wise papers of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics & Biology, besides, giving you an option to take our All India test series. The test taking experience is seamless, as the test taking does not require internet connectivity. Here is a brief overview about what you can find inside the App: - Free Chapter Wise Practice Tests segregated according to difficulty level - Time Based Topic Wise Tests as well as Full Tests - Detailed reports after each test analysing your performance - Relative performance indicating your strengths and weaknesses - All India Test Series predicting your rank at state and national level - Detailed high quality solutions, Counselling and Personalized guidance by expert IITians
