RECRUITMENT AND TRAINING

At RiSE fellowship program, we believe in creating future leaders amidst difficulties. We will recruit promising college graduates, with an age bar of 29 years, from diverse backgrounds, from the best colleges of India and abroad.

Prospective fellows need to complete the online application form and follow up with a multiple round of interviews. In a fellow, we will look for commitment and perseverance. Also, it will be greatly considered how the fellow plans to utilise his learnings and experience after completion of their fellowship.

Following recruitment, the selected fellows will undergo rigorous training which will be offered by experts from relevant fields. It will help them understand the condition and situation of students, connect with them and innovate different ways to streamline learning process.

WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR?


We look for working graduates or final/pre final year college students coming from diverse background to form a strong team of fellows. We want our fellows to have sheer determination & grit to solve problems through creative thinking and innovative solutions. Fellows are expected to make the best out of this opportunity at RiSE and scale the impact of their work and be the leader for tomorrow. Fellows should have strong perseverance with a sense of belonging to the workplace.
We can’t wait to listen from you about your desire to be a changemaker and inspire others.

APPREHENSIONS

In Kashmir, not only the people but the spectacular Himalayas yearn for someone who will help their children rise. It’s true that the valley is hit by a conflict, but The disturbance won’t intersect your visit any moment. Your stay and workplace will be provided at utmost safe and secured place. Despite the negativity that is majorly highlighted about Kashmir, it should be noted that Kashmir has one of the lowest crime rates among all states in India. Also, Kashmir is regarded as one of the safest places in the country for tourists. The kindness and warmth that Kashmir has to offer is a truly different experience of which you can be a part, that won’t be offered in any other part of the world. This valley of saints is an ocean of faith merged together with warmth and love. You will never feel alone when you watch the sunset sitting beside Dal lake. Welcome to the fellowship and you’re all that an enlightened Kashmir wants.
