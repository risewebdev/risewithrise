WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR?


We can’t wait to listen from you if you’re passionate to be a changemaker and bring out the best from brilliant Kashmiri minds. Are you are willing to make the best out of this opportunity at RiSE and scale the impact of your work and be the leader for tomorrow? Do you want to be committed for a single cause to uplift and take Kashmir to a new level and your passion to rectify, resolve and recommend is on the top? Then, this is the place for you. Come, be a part, not apart.

RECRUITMENT AND TRAINING

In the middle of every difficulty, lies an opportunity. At RiSE fellowship program, we believe to create future leaders from the conflict. We aim to recruit most promising college graduates from a diverse array of backgrounds including the nation’s best colleges.

Fellows applying need to complete the online application form and follow up with a multiple round of interviews. In the interviews, the primary thing to be tested is the level of commitment and perseverance in the fellow. Also, it will be greatly considered if the fellow proceeds to bring about the best changes in the near future.

Following recruitment, the selected fellows will undergo a rigorous training period which will be offered by experts from various fields. It will help them understand the condition and situation of students, connect with them and innovate different ways to make the process of learning friendly and much easier.
