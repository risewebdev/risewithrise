Kath Baath

Kath Baath, an initiative put forth by the future of tomorrow, is a first of its kind initiative in the valley, which is totally youth-oriented and dedicated for the betterment of future society.


.
Settled as a unique and a creative place for the youth of Kashmir, thoughts, opinions, and ideas are allowed to flourish which help build up strong voices for the generation to come. The topics discussed are on everyday problems, which the society usually tends to ignore as something too clichéd to be talked about. It is the only platform in Kashmir without any gender segregation where boys and girls come together in the same space to share their opinions. We at Kath Baath do not revolve around a single idea, but we break down the issue to create a simplified version and find its root cause. Being totally distraught with internet curbs and curfews, the youth do not get an opportunity to discuss their problems on an online platform and that is where Kath Baath has come to an aid of reviving the lost culture of debate and discourse in Kashmir by providing a platform to literary talent. Our generic discussions are based on certain issues like Girl Child Abuse, Gender Discrimination, and Girl Child Education, which the society doesn’t want to talk about and see the change in. Our organization aims to believe and stand for the motto which claims that “The story of tomorrow is hidden in the youth of today.”
