Vision

At RiSE, we believe that a successful classroom is one in which the students' zeal to learn is matched by the teacher's passion to teach. We lay emphasis on selecting the right set of students and providing them with coaching and personalized mentoring to help them meet their goals.

Fellowship Program

To handle the multi-folds problem and create a long lasting impact by touching more souls especially of the underprivileged section of the society - we introduce to you the RiSE fellowship. The RiSE Fellowship Program is an opportunity for every blazing mind to Educate, Collaborate and Empower.

RiSE vClass
RiSE is Kashmir’s first free online education platform with the aim of providing precise, compact and useful information for anyone, anywhere. With the RiSE channel, you can practice variety of numerical problems with comprehensive solutions and technique within the comfort of your home. More...

RiSE Android App.
Mobile APP: Curated access to our content & counseling materials for underprivileged students in remote areas; ~50,000 students. App used for online assessments & video lectures; acting as a workaround during frequent curfews, strikes & riots. More...

Classroom Program

Team RiSE aims at providing comprehensive preparatory classes to improve the capability and quality of education in Kashmir. We aim to help students in qualifying prestigious exams like JEE, SAT, NEET, AIIMS, etc. Our main motive of providing education is through compact learning, which should be impactful. More...

Fellowship

The RiSE fellowship has an entirely different way of pursuing experience and getting an opportunity to teach and mentor the conflict-hit Kashmiris. Despite the steeper challenges and irregularity in the academic session, very bright kids from different areas, rural or urban, never fail to amaze us with their creativity and intellect. More...

Talent Search

Tests were conducted across all 10 districts of Kashmir. Students of class 10th, 11th & 12th were eligible to participate. Test was based on NCERT syllabus and general aptitude. It is a flash test and no preparation required. Based on the test, meritorious students will be identified in the following disciplines. More...

Kath Baath

Kath Baath, an initiative put forth by the future of tomorrow, is a first of its kind initiative in the valley, which is totally youth-oriented and dedicated for the betterment of future society. It is the only platform in Kashmir without any gender segregation where boys and girls come together in the same space to share their opinions. More...
Stay informed & well-prepared.

Adequate and appropriate guidance is necessary to achieve success.
At RISE, we keep a track of regular changes that are made to the patterns of competitive examinations held nation wide. We apprise our students on how to deal with the challenges arising due to such changes.

Test yourself. Strive for excellence.

Regular, topic wise and entrance pattern tests are held to ensure students stay focussed & develop the right kind of mindset towards competitive examinations. Strategies are suggested to students for them to improve and master the art of performing to their potential.

Personalized attention.

At RISE, limited batch size ensures personalized attention to each and every one of the students.
Regular one-on-one sessions with the students ensures that all the difficulties and concerns of students are addressed in a timely manner. The faculty is available round the clock to help students with all sorts of issues.
