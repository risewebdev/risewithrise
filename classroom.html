Classroom Programs

Team RiSE aims at providing comprehensive preparatory classes
to improve the capability and quality of education in Kashmir.

We aim to help students in qualifying prestigious exams like JEE, SAT, NEET, AIIMS, etc.
Our main motive of providing education is through compact learning, which should be impactful.


Believing in the method of equal importance for learning and self-studying, we conduct classes 3-4 days a week, which helps students to grasp, learn and practice accordingly.


With a belief of having personalized interaction with the students, we have a maximum ratio of 1:30 in our classrooms for students to have better coordination and adaptability with the class fellows and the tutors.




At RiSE, we aim to mentor students 24x7 by providing assignments and doubt clearances through our online learning programs.


Adequate and appropriate guidance is necessary to achieve success. At RISE, we keep a track of regular changes that are made to the patterns of competitive examinations held nation wide. We apprise our students on how to deal with the challenges arising due to such changes.


Regular, topic wise and entrance pattern tests are held to ensure students stay focussed & develop the right kind of mindset towards competitive examinations. Strategies are suggested to students for them to improve and master the art of performing to their potential.


At RISE, limited batch size ensures personalized attention to each and every one of the students. Regular one-on-one sessions with the students ensures that all the difficulties and concerns of students are addressed in a timely manner.
