THE PROBLEM

Quality education is a sparse commodity in Kashmir. The decades of conflict and uncertainty has left the education system in tatters. Owing to complete negligence from the administration, the schooling system has suffered the most over the years. As a result, students, instead of deciding on what opportunities to pursue, have been pushed towards hopelessness. Local youth are not able to acquire the knowledge and training for their academic/professional growth and development. They are not being transformed into quality professionals. Consequently, the representation of students from Kashmir in premier educational institutions is minimal. This has created a massive problem of unemployment and underemployment among the youth. In the absence of adequate guidance, counselling and mentors, students perform poorly in examinations, and lack the necessary awareness about the educational opportunities they could avail. The combination of lack of awareness and guidance, over the years, has prevented students in Kashmir from dreaming big, and has pushed them towards mediocrity.

HOW AT RiSE WE STARTED SOLVING THE PROBLEM


The privilege to dream for a better future has been denied to the youth of Kashmir since decades. To think about getting an admission in prestigious institutions seemed no more like a fairy-tale to every student. At RiSE, we started to ignite the hopes and boost the morale of students and made them realize their capabilities to excel and achieve success. We impart required skills and knowledge through the most effective learning process. Over the course of years, many of our students got into prestigious institutions and became role models for the Kashmir youth. We learned to create community support and worked according to mindset and requirements of people.

At RISE, we equalize educational opportunities for students, by bridging the gap between talent and opportunities through robust solutions. In the process, we are creating change-makers and future leaders.

At RISE, we plan to create future leaders and change makers.

THE FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM

RiSE fellowship provides an opportunity to talented youth to embark on an enriching journey, full of challenges and learning, to work towards improving the quality of education in Kashmir valley. To begin with an enhanced and well oriented community of mentors, The RiSE Fellowship follows a three-tier model: Educate, Collaborate & Empower, to introduce change and bring success.

Educate: Fellows will be involved in teaching subjects of their expertise to improve core academics of students of class 9th and 10th, especially from the underprivileged section of the society. They will also conduct plethora of intricately designed activities to enhance life skills set and impart confidence.

Collaborate: The problems are both, deeply rooted and multi folded, for which the solution can’t be one-dimensional. To tackle such challenges, the fellows will get an opportunity to collaborate with stakeholders in and around their workplaces and come up with solutions to improve the quality of education. Being role models for students, fellows will be able to create a bond with them, where in they’ll learn about their specific problems, their issues and help tackle them.

Empower: Fellows will constantly motivate and inspire students to chase excellence to become future leaders. They will be the doorway to a whole new world of exposure and opportunities which can help many to dream big and hope for a better tomorrow.

In the entire process, our fellows will learn the essence of leadership, and realize that how they as a unit can drive change and transform lives. The fellowship is for a period of 6 months and is a lifetime opportunity to bring about a meaningful change, in others as well as yourself.

IMPACT OF FELLOWSHIP

Our work and our vale is an island of hope in the ocean of hopelessness. To deal and work with such a community is a new thing in itself and crossing all these hurdles over the entire course of fellowship is what makes this fellowship a transformational journey for you to learn, and unfold a world of amazing opportunities.

Innovation and critical thinking: The fellows working with us will develop an ability to analyze various unprecedented situations and introduce different techniques and processes of educating. The real challenge for fellows is to find a way to make their innovative ideas socially acceptable and practically implementable.

Commitment: By working with the students, a sense of dedication and responsibility will be imbibed and as a teacher and coach our fellows will earn the art of listening, counseling, and motivating.

Ability to work independently: Since, RiSE fellowship programme is first of its kind, every fellow will get to know about each and everything from the scratch. Entrepreneurship skill enhancement, adapting to social and political turbulence, problems solving are some of the features of the fellowship.

At RiSE fellowship, the fellows become the faces of the organisation. They not only get to conduct classes but they are provided with an opportunity to explore all the factors affecting the entire education system of a particular region. Walk this journey full of unmatched opportunities.

HOW WE TAKE CARE OF OUR FELLOWS

Fellows will receive a monthly salary of INR 20,000 in addition to food and accommodation facility. They will also be provided with a to and fro air ticket from Delhi to Srinagar. Not to mention, this opportunity is a great addition to your resume and make you stand out with a life changing experience.
