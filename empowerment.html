Success Stories

By generating these success stories RiSE has created a wave of hope in the valley.
There is an exponential increment in the number of students applying to foreign universities and appearing in competitive examinations.

RiSE Android App.

Created access to our content & counseling materials for underprivileged students in remote areas; ~50,000 students.
App used for online assessments acting as a workaround during frequent curfews, strikes & riots.

RiSE vClass

More than 200,000 YouTube views of RiSE video lectures.
Providing classroom experience in the native language of students who cannot afford joining any institute at the comfort and safety of their homes.

Experiential learning

Kath Bath, an idea of some students studying at RiSE which was molded into an organization under the guidance of faculties at RiSE is now officially a part of Ashoka India.
Kath Baath is among the very few ideas across the country and the first from Kashmir that has made the cut into Ashoka's Youth Venture.
